Five young friends escape Kathmandu in a rugged old Land Rover, chasing music, freedom, and unresolved emotions on a road trip to Pokhara only to discover that the journey changes them more than the destination ever could.

Main Characters.

Sushant

Musician, guitar player, owner of the Land Rover. Struggles between Kathmandu stability and artistic freedom.

Mona

Bold, sensual energy. Represents chaos, freedom, and emotional unpredictability.

Sushil

DIY survivalist, protector. Represents raw masculinity, fearlessness, and loyalty.

Manav

Lead vocalist in Pokhara. Emotionally distant, waiting for reconciliation.

Divya

Mysterious solo traveler. Brings fresh perspective and emotional disruption to the group.
